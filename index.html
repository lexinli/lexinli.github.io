Bio

Lexin Li, Ph.D., is Professor of Biostatistics, and Division Chair, at Department of Biostatistics and Epidemiology, and also Department of Statistics and Helen Wills Neuroscience Institute, at the University of California, Berkeley. His research interests include neuroimaging analysis, brain-computer-interface, deep and reinforcement learning, functional and point process data analysis, tensor data analysis, and brain network analysis. He is a Fellow of the American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS), the Institute of Mathematical Statistics (IMS), the American Statistical Association (ASA), the Asia-Pacific Artificial Intelligence Association (AAIA), and an Elected Member of the International Statistical Institute (ISI). He is the Editor-in-Chief of the Annals of Applied Statistics for 2025-27.
